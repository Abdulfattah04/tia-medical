we understand that quality healthcare is more than just treatment; it’s about trust, comfort, and convenience. Our friendly and highly skilled team is dedicated to ensuring every patient feels welcome and valued from the moment they arrive.

Preventive Health Screenings

Our preventive health screenings are designed to help you stay proactive about your health. From routine blood pressure and cholesterol checks to early detection screenings for diabetes and cancer, we ensure that you have the knowledge you need to maintain optimal health.

Specialist Consultations

Our clinic provides access to a wide array of specialists, including cardiologists, endocrinologists, and orthopedists, among others. Our specialists are here to address specific health concerns, offering tailored consultations and treatments to meet your unique needs.

Pediatric Care

We are committed to providing top-quality healthcare for children, from newborns to teenagers. Our pediatric services include regular check-ups, vaccinations, developmental assessments, and treatment for acute and chronic illnesses, all in a caring, child-friendly environment.

Women’s Health Services

Our women’s health services are designed to support every stage of life. We offer gynecological exams, family planning, prenatal and postnatal care, and specialized care for conditions such as PCOS, endometriosis, and menopause management.

Chronic Disease Management

For patients with chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, or asthma, we create personalized care plans focused on long-term wellness. Our team provides regular monitoring, lifestyle guidance, and medication management to help you lead a fulfilling life.

Vaccinations and Immunizations

Protecting yourself and your loved ones against preventable diseases is vital. We provide routine vaccinations, including flu shots, travel vaccines, and immunizations for children, teens, and adults. Our clinic follows the latest health guidelines to keep you safe and healthy.

Located in the heart of Cairo, Tia Medical Clinic is a trusted healthcare provider committed to delivering patient-centered, high-quality medical services.
