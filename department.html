Departments

General Medicine / Internal Medicine

General Health Management: Diagnoses and treats a wide range of non-specialized conditions, including respiratory infections, gastrointestinal issues, and minor injuries. Internists provide routine check-ups and manage acute illnesses.

Preventive Care & Chronic Disease Management: Conducts health screenings (e.g., blood pressure, cholesterol), advises on lifestyle changes, and manages chronic diseases like diabetes, hypertension, and asthma to prevent complications.

Read more

Gynecology & Obstetrics (OB-GYN)

Women’s Reproductive Health Care: Focuses on overall reproductive health, including menstrual disorders, hormonal imbalances, and conditions like polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) and endometriosis. OB-GYNs also perform Pap smears, pelvic exams, and breast exams.

Prenatal, Pregnancy, and Postpartum Care: Provides prenatal care to monitor the health of both mother and fetus, performs routine ultrasounds, and offers counseling on diet and lifestyle for a healthy pregnancy. They continue care through delivery and assist with postpartum recovery.

Read more

Dermatology

Skin, Hair, and Nail Condition Treatment: Manages a variety of skin disorders like eczema, psoriasis, and fungal infections, as well as cosmetic concerns like acne and hair loss. Dermatologists offer topical treatments, medications, and therapeutic procedures.

Skin Screenings and Minor Procedures: Conducts screenings for skin cancer and other abnormalities, removes moles and cysts, and treats conditions like warts with minor outpatient procedures.
