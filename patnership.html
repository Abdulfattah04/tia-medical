Partnerships

HMO Partnerships
At Tia Medical Clinic, we partner with top Health Maintenance Organizations (HMOs) to offer our patients affordable, high-quality healthcare services. We currently accept:

MisrCare

With a commitment to making healthcare accessible, MisrCare offers comprehensive health insurance plans designed to cover a wide range of medical needs. At Tia Medical Clinic, MisrCare members have access to our full suite of healthcare services, from routine check-ups to specialist consultations, all at an affordable cost.

Medicare Egypt

As one of Egypt’s leading health insurance providers, Medicare Egypt focuses on providing quality care and peace of mind. Tia Medical Clinic proudly accepts Medicare Egypt, ensuring that members receive trusted healthcare services tailored to their needs, including preventive screenings, chronic disease management, and pediatric care.

Prime Health

Prime Health is dedicated to providing flexible healthcare solutions with extensive coverage options. By partnering with Prime Health, Tia Medical Clinic offers accessible healthcare services for Prime Health members, ensuring that essential health services remain both affordable and convenient.

AXA Egypt

AXA Egypt is known for its focus on innovative, customer-focused health plans. As a Tia Medical Clinic partner, AXA Egypt members can enjoy seamless access to quality care, including our advanced diagnostic services, women’s health, and chronic disease management.

Allianz Care

A globally trusted provider, Allianz Care offers comprehensive coverage to patients seeking high-standard healthcare. Our partnership allows Allianz Care members to benefit from Tia Medical Clinic’s state-of-the-art facilities and wide range of healthcare services, from vaccinations to specialized consultations.

Bupa Egypt

As a leading international health insurance provider, Bupa Egypt offers extensive coverage options and a commitment to patient well-being. Through our partnership, Bupa Egypt members can access the full range of Tia Medical Clinic’s healthcare services, including preventive screenings, vaccinations, specialist consultations, and chronic disease management.

Located in the heart of Cairo, Tia Medical Clinic is a trusted healthcare provider committed to delivering patient-centered, high-quality medical services.
