History Of TIA Medical Clinic

Founded with a vision of excellence and a commitment to compassionate healthcare, Tia Medical Clinic has grown to become a trusted name in Cairo’s healthcare landscape. Established in the heart of Egypt, Tia Medical Clinic was born out of a desire to make high-quality medical care accessible and affordable for individuals and families alike. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include a wide range of specialties, from preventive health screenings to chronic disease management, all while maintaining a patient-first approach.
